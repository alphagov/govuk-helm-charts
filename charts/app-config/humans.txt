Today, we say farewell to Jason Birchall, our Lead SRE on GOV.UK Platform Engineering.

Jason has spent a hard 18 months with GOV.UK, helping us to improve the Platform and our ways
of working. He came over from the Ministry of Justice having worked on their Platform and
gave us new ideas for how to make the GOV.UK Platform even better.

Jason's directness and honesty has pushed us to become better engineers and helped us to drive
towards a healthier Platform Engineering culture. He has placed users at the centre of our work;
showing us how to have an open mind and help us remember that users can mean a lot of different
things.

GDS are going to miss Jason dearly and wish him well in his future endeavours!

GOV.UK is built by a team at the Government Digital Service (with thanks to Martha!) in London,
Bristol and Manchester.  If you’d like to join us, see https://gdscareers.gov.uk/
